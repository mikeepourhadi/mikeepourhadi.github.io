What's your favorite way to make a breakfast burrito?

  1. Heat a skillet on medium
  2. Take two whole eggs, pour crack into cup
  3. Whisk, but only for a few seconds
  4. Put some cheese on a tortilla
  5. Place your tortilla in a toaster oven
  6. Cook your scrambled eggs (don't season until last few seconds of cooking)
  7. DO NOT STOP STIRRING THE EGGS UNTIL THEY ARE DONE
  8. Remove tortilla, place on plate
  9. Place leafy green (I use kale) on tortilla
  10. Place scrambled eggs on plate
  11. Drizzle on preferred salsa
  12. Eat
  13. Enjoy the best moments of your life
